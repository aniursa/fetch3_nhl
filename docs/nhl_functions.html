nhl_transpiration.NHL_functions.calc_mixing_length(z, h, alpha=0.1)¶

Calculates the mixing length for each height in z Based on Poggi et al 2004 Zero-plane displacement height is taken as (2/3)*h, appropriate for dense canopies (Katul et al 2004)

z : vector of heights [m] h : canopy height [m] alpha : unitless parameter

mixing_length : mixing length [m] at each height in z

nhl_transpiration.NHL_functions.calc_zenith_angle(doy, lat, long, time_offset, time_of_day)¶

Calculates the solar zenith angle, based on Campbell & Norman, 1998

doy : Day of year (Ordinal day, e.g. 1 = Jan 1) lat : Latitude long : Longitude (Needs to be negative for deg W, positive for deg E) time_offset : Time offset [in hours] for local standard time zone, e.g, for Pacific Standard Time, time_offset = -8 time_of_day : Time of day (hours) in local standard time

Note: Be sure that time of day and time offset are in local standard time, not daylight savings

zenith_angle_deg : zenith angle of the sun [degrees]

nhl_transpiration.NHL_functions.solve_Uz(z, dz, Cd, a_s, U_top, **kwargs)¶

Solves the momentum equation to calculate the vertical wind profile. Applies no-slip boundary condition: wind speed = 0 at surface (z = 0). Model for turbulent diffusivity of momentum is from Poggi 2004, eqn 6

z : vector of heights [m] mixing_length : mixing length [d] at each height in z Cd : drag coefficient [unitless], assumed to be 0.2 (Katul et al 2004) a_s: leaf surface area [m2] U_top : Measured wind speed at top of canopy [m s-1] **kwargs to be passed to calc_mixing_length

Km : turbulent diffusivity of momentum at each height in z [m s-1] U : wind speed at each height in z [m s-1]
